- Classification: predict a category.
- Regression: predict a number.

Keep this page focused on the task distinction, not on individual algorithms.
Algorithms belong on the child task pages and their lesson pages.

## Current teaching frame

Supervised learning is framed as learning from labeled examples:

1. Read labeled examples.
2. Compare predictions with known answers.
3. Generalize to new examples.

The key idea is that labeled history gives the model a way to practice before
it sees unlabeled future cases.
